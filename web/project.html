PROJECT EXPO

                                                                                                ** WE WILL PROVIDE LUNCH FOR THE BELOW EVENT **

Over the past 50 years human population has nearly tripled and the effect of these is visible on the environment. Carbon emissions have reached record level of 31.6 Giga tonnes. People across globe are looking at innovative ways for society to become ecologically responsible.

The solution is to go digital and reduce carbon foot print. The effective utilization of technology helps reduce carbon foot print.

Present a prototype on the below topics:

1. Energy efficiency – efficient utilization of energy

2. Smart agriculture

3. Ambient assisted living

4. Smart homes

Internet of Things or IoT is an environment or network where everything and everyone are connected. Internet of Things is growing rapidly with more devices getting connected every day. There are many areas of applications of the Internet of Things like consumer, health, industrial, transportation, security, entertainment and many other. Projects on any of these would be acceptable to compete.

Students, Innovators, Corporates, Industrialists and all other enthusiastic people from all kind of background are invited to exhibit their product or innovation. We aim to highlight the technologies that reach out to critical social needs of the country, also simultaneously satisfy the requirements of industry and maximize the benefit to society.

Project Report Submission:

The project report should be mailed to vidhyutra2k17@gmail.com with the subject Project Expo: Project Report: Team ID (For example Project Expo: Project Report: IO1234). Teams must follow the following details for the submission:

1. The abstract must be submitted in word format only

2. Font: Verdana

3. Size: 11

4. Spacing between two lines: 6 pts

5. Spacing between two paragraphs: 10 pts

6. Bottom margin: 1 inch

General Rules:

1. The competition is open to all college students, research scholars and budding start-ups. All projects being displayed will have a fair chance of receiving further development opportunities offered by funding organizations and Venture capitalists.

2. Every team has to register online on our website for the competition. A registration number will be allocated to the team on registration which shall be used for future references.

3. A team can register at any point of time before 18th January, 2017 and can submit final abstract and video (as mentioned above).

4. The decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding on all. Vidhyutra-2k17 has all the rights to verify the identity and accuracy of the details provided by the participants.

5. No responsibility will be held by Vidhyutra-2k17, GVPCOE (A) for any late, lost or misdirected entries.

6. Note that at any point of time the latest information will be that which is on the website. However, registered participants will be informed through mail about any changes on the website.

Evaluation Criteria:

Projects will be judged by a panel of experts. Following are the broad guidelines for judging:

1. Innovation and creativity in the method used or product developed will be given high weightage.

2. Implement ability of the product to be assessed on the following parameters:

a. Technical feasibility

b. Cost of the product

c. Usability

d. Acceptability

e. Market Value and acceptance

f. Reliability/ durability of the product or method developed

3. Performance

In case of any dispute, decision of the organizers or Judges will final and binding on all.

Certificate Policy:

Only those teams that are shortlisted for the finals and also give a final presentation about their work during Vidhyutra-2k17 would be awarded a Certificate of Participation. The top entries from this event would be provided with Certificate of Excellence

Team Specification:

The participating entries must be in a team of a maximum of 3[three] people. If the participating team feels that their idea requires more participants in their team, they can forward their request, with suitable reasons, to vidhyutra2k17@gmail.com with the subject "Project Expo: team number increase".

Fee: INR. 150/- per team. Maximum of 3 member(s) per team
